Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between retainer and one-time?

Monthly Retainer: Lower upfront, includes ongoing maintenance, unlimited support, daily backups, security updates, and growth optimizations.

One-Time Purchase: Pay once, own everything forever. We transfer the GitHub repository to you. You host it yourself and manage your own updates.

Can I cancel or get a refund?

No. Once work commences (within 1 hour of payment), no cancellations and no refunds are available under any circumstances. This includes design fees, monthly retainers, and one-time purchases. Please review our Terms of Service before purchasing.

Can I switch from monthly to one-time?

Yes, you may request to switch. The one-time purchase price will be the full amount minus credit for your design fee only. Monthly retainer payments do not count toward one-time pricing. Contact us for a custom quote.

How does GitHub transfer work for one-time purchase?

Once payment is confirmed and cleared, we create a private GitHub repository with your website code, then transfer full ownership to your GitHub account. Transfer typically completes within 14 business days.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

We provide unlimited revisions during the design phase to ensure your satisfaction. Once you approve the final deliverable, the project is considered complete. No refunds are issued after work commences.

Do I own the code in both plans?

Yes. In retainer plans, you own the design after paying the design fee. In one-time, you own everything immediately after payment. Full details in our Terms of Service.
