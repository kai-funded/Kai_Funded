Privacy Policy

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any details you provide when contacting us or placing an order.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process orders and deliver services
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our website and offerings

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed through Stripe. Stripe may collect and store your data according to their own privacy practices, which you can view on their website.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and monitor site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Protection

Your personal data is stored securely and is not shared, sold, or disclosed to any third party without your consent, except as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletions of personal data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at thekaifunded@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
